How we got the data

Every university in the United States, public or private, is mandated by the Clery Act to report crime that occurs near or on its campus.

To obtain the daily crime log for your university, you need to call the campus police's records office, the school’s public safety office, or the campus records-keeper and ask for this data. They'll likely ask you to put your request in writing via email, but it’s good to call first.

Here's an example email:

Dear Record Keeper’s name,

My name is Your Name, and I'm a student at Your University. I'm doing a data project about college crime. I am wondering if you can help me get access to the daily campus crime log from the past 5 years at Your University. I’m hoping to receive this data as soon as possible.

I’m specifically looking for:

  • Your University's daily crime log from the past 5 years
  • sent electronically in the form of a spreadsheet, CSV file, or an accessible API
  • with the nature of the crime, location, time, and status of the incident
If the past 5 years are not possible, I would like whatever you can give me from the past 2 to 5 years.

I'd also like to be CC’d on any messages that you forward on this matter. Please let me know if I can clarify any aspects of my request for you. Thank you again for helping me access this data and for your timely response.

Sincerely,

Your Name

Technically, this counts as a public records request, and the state rules regarding these types of requests will apply.

Before sending your email, research the public records rules in your state and make sure to mention them in the beginning of your email. For example, pursuant to government code I am requesting daily crime logs from university. Please respond within mandated time, as required by government code. If the cost of this request exceeds $50, please notify me.

If they don’t respond or give you incomplete data, keep pushing. They are required by law to make this data accessible to anyone who requests it—you don’t have to be a journalist or a professional.

If they give you everything you asked for right away–congratulations! Now you can get started on creating this web app for your college campus.
